Career Information Hub

📚 Academic & Pure Science

Degrees: B.Sc. Honours, BS, Integrated BS-MS

Institutions: IISc, IISERs, NISER, TIFR, Delhi University, CMI

Exams: CUET-UG, JEST, GATE, JRF

Careers: Research Scientist, University Professor, Laboratory Head

⚙️ Technical & Engineering

Degrees: B.Tech, B.E., M.Tech, Ph.D.

Institutions: IITs, NITs, BITS Pilani, DTU

Exams: JEE Advanced, JEE Main, BITSAT

Careers: R&D Engineer, Aerospace Engineer, Robotics Engineer

🏥 Medical & Allied Health

Degrees: B.Sc. Medical Tech, M.Sc. Medical Physics

Institutions: AIIMS, Medical Colleges, Cancer Centers

Exams: NEET-UG, AIIMS Institutional

Careers: Medical Physicist, Radiation Safety Officer, Radiotherapist

✈️ Vocational & Applied Tech

Degrees: Commercial Pilot License, B.Sc. Aviation, B.Sc. Nautical Science

Institutions: IGRUA, IMU Campuses, Flying Academies

Exams: IMU-CET, IGRUA Test, CUET-UG

Careers: Commercial Pilot, Merchant Navy Officer, GIS Analyst
